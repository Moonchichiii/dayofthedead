Reasons why

I created a website to share my passion for the Day of the Dead festival, a colorful and meaningful celebration.

Initially, I had planned to make a website about Alexander the Great's mother. However, I couldn't find any pictures online, which stressed me out, so I looked for other topics to explore.

As I reflected on what inspired me, I remembered my travels to Mexico over a decade ago, and the vivid memories of the Day of the Dead celebrations.

The idea stuck with me, and I decided to create a website to share the history, traditions, and beauty of this unique and fascinating festival.

Documentary

This documentary delves into the history and evolution of the Mexican Day of the Dead, tracing its roots from pagan traditions to the multifaceted observances of today.

Viewers are taken on a journey through the perspectives of artist Betsabee Romero, curator Laura Osorio Sunnucks, and paleobiologist Omar Regalado Fernandez,
as they explore the ways in which the living remember and honor the dead. This includes cooking, eating, drinking, music-making, and decorating graves.

The documentary emphasizes the significance of the "spirit of things" and the love that is shared in these rituals.
